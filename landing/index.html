Features

Everything you need,
nothing you don't

Built for professionals who want a real edge in every meeting, without anyone knowing it's there.

Screen-capture proof
Uses Windows WDA_EXCLUDEFROMCAPTURE to make the overlay invisible to Zoom, Teams, Google Meet, OBS, and every screen-recording API.
Real-time transcription
Captures system audio via WASAPI loopback — no meeting bot, no participant list entry. Deepgram nova-2 delivers <300ms transcription at 95%+ accuracy.
Instant AI responses
Press Ctrl+Enter for an instant, context-aware response from GPT-4o-mini — streamed token by token with no waiting.
Auto meeting notes
One click generates structured notes with a summary, key decisions, action items with owners, and open questions — ready to paste into Notion or Slack.
Auto-assist mode
Enable auto-ask and Ghostnote responds automatically after each utterance — proactively surfacing insights, suggested follow-ups, and next actions.
Screen context
Toggle screen capture to give the AI visual context — it reads your slides, documents, or shared content alongside the audio for richer, more relevant answers.
How it works

Up and running in 60 seconds

No integrations. No meeting bot setup. No SDK installation. Just download and go.

01
Download & launch
Download the .exe, sign in, and the overlay appears — already positioned and ready.
02
Join your meeting normally
Open Zoom, Teams, or Meet as usual. Hit Record in Ghostnote and it silently captures system audio in the background.
03
Ask anything mid-meeting
Press Ctrl+Enter or tap a quick-action chip. Ghostnote reads the live transcript and replies instantly.
04
Generate notes & save
When the meeting ends, tap Notes to generate a clean summary. Sessions are saved to your history for future reference.
Privacy & Security

Built for privacy
from the ground up

Ghostnote operates entirely at the OS level. There is no cloud backend, no meeting SDK, and no persistent audio storage. What happens in your meeting stays on your machine.

Audit the source code
Overlay hidden via WDA_EXCLUDEFROMCAPTURE — invisible to all screen-sharing and recording software
Audio captured via WASAPI loopback — no microphone injection, no meeting SDK presence
Transcription streamed over encrypted WebSocket to Deepgram — never stored permanently
Hide overlay instantly with Ctrl+Shift+H — one keystroke, gone in milliseconds
Open source — every line of code is public and auditable on GitHub
