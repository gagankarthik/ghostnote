How it works

Up and running in 60 seconds.

No meeting bot, no calendar access, no browser extension. Just a lightweight app on your PC.

1
Download & launch
Install Ghostnote on your Windows PC. It starts as a small floating overlay — invisible to everyone but you.
2
Join your meeting
Open Zoom, Teams, or any platform as normal. Hit Record in Ghostnote and it begins listening silently.
3
Ask AI
Press Ctrl+Enter or tap a quick action. Ghostnote reads the full conversation and delivers a precise answer instantly.
4
Respond with confidence
Read the answer on your screen — invisible to others — and speak confidently. Save notes when you're done.

Privacy & security

Built for
confidential conversations.

Ghostnote was designed from the ground up for high-stakes situations — job interviews, investor calls, client meetings — where privacy is everything.

Zero-persistence design
No files are written to disk. No conversation history leaves your device without your explicit action. When your session ends, everything resets automatically.
No meeting bots or integrations
Ghostnote doesn't join your meeting as a participant, access your calendar, or connect to any platform API. It simply listens to audio you already hear.
Fully open source
Every line of Ghostnote's code is publicly available on GitHub. Review exactly what it does — no hidden behavior, no telemetry, no surprises.
FAQ

Common questions.

Can't find your answer? Open a GitHub issue and we'll respond quickly.

Can my interviewer see Ghostnote on screen share?
No. Ghostnote is completely excluded from screen share at the operating system level. It is invisible to Zoom, Teams, Google Meet, and any recording software — your screen looks 100% normal to everyone else.
Does it need a bot to join the meeting?
No meeting bot, no calendar link, no platform integration required. Ghostnote works by listening to the audio already playing through your system — privately and locally, with no meeting access whatsoever.
How accurate are the AI answers?
Ghostnote uses a state-of-the-art AI model with the full context of your meeting — everything said up to that point. Answers are specific, structured, and ready to say out loud immediately.
Is this ethical to use?
Ghostnote is a personal productivity tool, similar to having notes open beside you. It's designed to help you communicate more confidently and effectively. How you use it is your responsibility.
What operating systems does it support?
Currently Windows only. The screen-share invisibility feature relies on a Windows-native technology that hardware-excludes the overlay from all capture APIs — a capability not available on other platforms.
Is Ghostnote free?
Yes. Ghostnote is fully open source and free to download. You provide your own AI and transcription API keys — no subscription, no usage fees from us, ever.
